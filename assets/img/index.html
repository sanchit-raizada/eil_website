What Sets Us Apart

Fashion that Fits Your Budget

At Simran International, we believe that premium leather fashion should be accessible.

Our design and manufacturing teams create contemporary leather products that balance style, durability and comfort—while remaining cost-effective for global brands and retailers.

From concept development to finished leather products, every stage is handled with strict quality control and efficient production processes.

This allows us to deliver affordable luxury leather products without compromising on material quality, finishing standards or customer expectations.

A Responsibility We Wear with Pride

At Simran International, sustainability is not an add-on. It is an integral part of how we operate.

We follow responsible sourcing practices to ensure that our leather supply chain supports:

  • ethical procurement
  • reduced environmental impact
  • safe working conditions
  • fair labour practices

Our manufacturing processes are designed to minimize waste, optimize resource usage and comply with international environmental and social standards.

We believe that modern leather manufacturing must protect both people and the planet—while continuing to meet the performance needs of global fashion brands.

More Than Just Leather: It's a Feeling

Every product created at Simran International represents more than material and craftsmanship.

It reflects:

  • confidence in quality
  • comfort in everyday use
  • reliability in performance
  • and timeless design value

Our leather products are designed to become a natural part of everyday life—supporting personal style, professional identity and long-term usability.

At Simran International, leather is not just a product category. It is an experience shaped by design, engineering, craftsmanship and responsibility.

50 Years

Completed In Leather Industry

With more than 50 years of experience in the leather industry, Simran International has built a strong reputation as a reliable global leather supplier and manufacturing partner. Since 1972, we have continuously evolved our production capabilities, technology adoption and quality systems to serve international markets. Today, we proudly work with 97 international brands and supply to over 10,000 retail points worldwide, including key markets such as: Spain, Italy, England, Switzerland, Netherlands, Germany, France, Denmark, Australia and Russia. Our global exposure allows us to understand regional fashion trends, regulatory standards and customer preferences—making Simran International a dependable partner for international leather sourcing.
