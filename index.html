About EIL

Powering India's Energy Future

Engineers India Limited (EIL) is a premier Engineering Consultancy and EPC company under the Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas. Since 1965, we have delivered comprehensive project solutions from concept to commissioning across the globe.

Fortune 500 Clients

Trusted by world's leading energy majors across 27 countries.
